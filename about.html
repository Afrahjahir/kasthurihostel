Our Story

Kasthuri Women’s Hostel was established in 2002 by Mrs. Ramana and Mrs. Jamuna, inspired by their mother, the late Mrs. Kasthuri. Coming from a humble middle-class background, Mrs. Kasthuri supported her family through a small pickle business and later started her own restaurant on ECR Road. Her vision was to create a safe and comfortable living space for working women away from home. Motivated by this noble dream, her daughters found the hostel in her memory. Today, the hostel continues to fulfill Mrs. Kasthuri’s vision and is successfully managed by its proprietrix, Mrs. Durga Devi. Over the years, we’ve built multiple branches that combine modern facilities, professional management, and warm hospitality. Our goal has always been to make your hostel life enjoyable, stress-free, and memorable.
