About Kasthuri Hostel

Your Trusted Place to Stay in the Heart of Chennai

Welcome to Kasthuri Hostel, where comfort meets convenience and care. With over a decade of experience in offering safe and affordable accommodation, we’ve earned the trust of countless students and working professionals across Chennai.

Established in 2012 with our T. Nagar branch, we have since expanded to CIT Nagar and Nandanam, providing secure, homely spaces designed for peaceful living. Whether you’re searching for the best women’s hostel in T. Nagar or the best men’s hostel in Nandanam, Kasthuri Hostel ensures a premium stay experience at an affordable price.

Our Story

Kasthuri Women’s Hostel was established in 2002 by Mrs. Ramana and Mrs. Jamuna, inspired by their mother, the late Mrs. Kasthuri.

Coming from a humble background, Mrs. Kasthuri supported her family through a small pickle business and later opened her own restaurant on ECR Road. Her vision was to create a safe and comfortable living space for working women away from home. Motivated by her dream, her daughters founded the hostel in her memory.

Today, the hostel proudly continues her legacy under the leadership of Mrs. Durga Devi, the current proprietrix. Over the years, Kasthuri Hostel has grown into multiple branches that blend modern amenities, professional management, and warm hospitality.

Our goal remains simple — to make your hostel life comfortable, stress-free, and memorable.

Our Mission

To provide safe, comfortable, and affordable accommodation that feels like home, while maintaining the highest standards of hospitality and hygiene.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Chennai’s most trusted and preferred choice for men’s and women’s hostels — offering the perfect balance of luxury, convenience, and care.

Our Commitment

A hostel should be more than just a place to stay — it should be a space where you feel secure, inspired, and connected.
