About Kasthuri Hostel

Kasthuri Hostel offers a welcoming and secure environment for students and professionals. With 4 branches across Chennai city, we cater to your comfort, convenience, and peace of mind:

  • 1 Women’s Hostel – A secure, comfortable, and supportive environment specifically for women, with all amenities tailored to ensure safety and convenience.
  • 3 Men’s Hostels – Designed for safety, privacy, and a friendly community where you can meet like-minded residents.
